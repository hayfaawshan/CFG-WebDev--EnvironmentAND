Sustainability Initiatives

NotCo

NotCo creates plant-based alternatives to different types of food such as milk and meat products. Using an AI, called ‘Giuseppe’ to create a database of plants with similar chemical properties to ingredients used in for example ‘mayo’ or ‘burger patties’ and can be used to generate a recipe that mimics the taste and texture.

Mara Elephant Project

The Mara Elephant Project is a protection organisation, who created an anti-poaching AI device called TrailGuard, using an algorithm to sift through pictures and only identify poachers using Machine Learning. Poachers kill around 35k African elephants every year and the species is only a decade away from extinction.

Suneeta Cosmetics

Suneeta Cosmetics are a vegan and ethical cosmetics company, based in London. Suneeta are committed to creating zero waste, plastic-free and eco-friendly beauty products, using ancient philosophies of Ayurveda in a modern way. Their products are unpackaged or have biodegradable packaging.

UpCircle

Upcircle are a beauty company creating effective products out of repurposed ingredients. Products such as their coffee body and face scrubs, made from used coffee grounds, to their natural face wash bars, made from repurposed Chai spices, their products help reduce waste in an innovative way. All products are also vegan and cruelty-free.

Macrebur Roads

Macrebur Roads are an innovative company who use waste plastic destined for landfill to create roads. From an idea born out of Southern India, Macrebur takes waste plastic to make asphalt roads, designed to meet worldwide road standards.
